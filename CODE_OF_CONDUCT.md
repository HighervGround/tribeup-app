# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors and users of TribeUp Social Sports App.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Focus on constructive feedback and collaboration
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate content
- Spam or off-topic discussions
- Personal attacks or inflammatory language
- Any behavior that creates an unwelcoming environment

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers directly.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will review all reports and take appropriate action, which may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans from the project.
